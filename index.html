How it works

The app every fisker owner absolutely needs

Version Check
Easily check all parts of your car to figure which versions you are on. This enables you to quickly identify parts that may require necessary updates.
Fault Check
Read our all recent fault checks of your car. Due proposed repair actions you get full control to partially fix things by yourself to have your car work.
Service actions
The heart of the app is the actual service actions. Execute actions like a T-Box reset, training nfc and key fobs, doggy windows and much much more.

How to get started

Its so easy, your mom could do it.

Get the Dongle
To be able to connect to your car, buy the vGate vLinker FD+ Dongle ie from here but may also be available on Amazon and other stores.
Setup Car + Browser
Connect the dongle to your car and set it ready. Use a WebBLE compatible browser - Chrome on Mac/Android or Microsoft Edge on Windows.
Start the App
Run the app any time on your mac, windows or android phone, let it connect to your car and enjoy all available tools. You can use it on multiple devices.
